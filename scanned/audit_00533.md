## Differences in Attribute Sources

There is a difference in the source of the attributes list utilized in the `get_attributes` function calls between `capy_labs::mix` and `genesis::mint`. The attribute `vec_map` in `capy_labs::mix` is obtained from the dynamic field of the `CapyLabsApp` object, while in `genesis::mint`, it is obtained from the dynamic field of the `Mint` object. This difference may result in unexpected behavior due to possible discrepancies in the attribute definitions within these objects.
