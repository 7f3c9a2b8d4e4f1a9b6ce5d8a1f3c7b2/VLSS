# Risk of Reentrancy During Flash Swap

## Summary
No summary provided.

## Details
## Vulnerability Report

In the pool, there is a lack of a reentrancy guard during flash swap operations, allowing potential reentrant calls to be made via other functions, which might result in the manipulation of the pool values.

## Remediation
Add a reentrancy guard to prevent the calling of other functions during the execution of a flash swap.

## Patch
Resolved in commit `f9025e9`.
