## OrderedVecSet and borrow_contents_mut

In the context of `ordered_vec_set`, `borrow_contents_mut` provides a mutable reference to the internal vector of `OrderedVecSet`. While this feature may be advantageous in specific scenarios, it also poses a potential risk of violating the invariants of `OrderedVecSet`, specifically, the requirement that the vector remains sorted in a descending order and contains unique elements.

> _sources/ordered_vec_set.move_

```rust
public fun borrow_contents(ord_vec: &OrderedVecSet): &vector<u128> {
    df::borrow(&ord_vec.id, Contents{})
}
```

## Remediation

Remove `borrow_contents_mut`.
